ABOUT

Industrial Learning Program is an initiative to provide students with opportunities to use their academic expertise in solving real-life problems. This would expose the students to applications of engineering in practical situations. Alumni from various sectors provide projects to the students to enhance and apply their knowledge and expertise. The projects are based on various problem statements and the students can apply for whichever problem intrigues them the most. The selected students get an opportunity to work under the guidance of the alumni.

RULEBOOK

ILP is conducted in the joint purview of the Student Alumni Relations Cell and Placement Cell. Students are required to sign IAF for taking up the project which will commence in the winters. Please go through the rule book very carefully before taking up the project. Here is the rule book which includes the procedure for signing up for the program and the rewards/penalties.

FAQ

There is a rule book attached above which clearly states all the steps to apply for the programme. The rewards/penalties are also clearly mentioned in it. Please go through the rule book very carefully.


The selection process will be totally dependent on the alumnus. We will send the resume to the alumnus who undertakes the screening on the basis of your resume or if required, a telephonic interview. The SARC team will contact you regarding the scheduling of interviews.


The duration will vary from project to project. In the list of the projects, there is a field that shows the duration of the project. In most cases, the projects are work from home, therefore, the project can also be continued during the semester.


Only one project can be taken by any student under ILP. Once the selection procedure is completed, you will be allotted the project for which you have been selected with due consideration of your preference.


The projects can be viewed only after registering on the portal. After successful registration, you can fill in your preferences for the projects.
